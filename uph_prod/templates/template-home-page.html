Health Ranking Dashboard

A comprehensive platform to measure the relative performance of districts with respect to the high-performance district (internal benchmarking) across health indicators.

The dashboard also helps district administration to identify the reasons for low performance (Availability, Quality and Utilization) and helps take appropriate actions, on a semi-annual basis.

CMO Dashboard

A digital platform to showcase different indicators, as decided by NITI Aayog, to track and monitor the performance of health care centers, which will further help in identifying key focus areas and develop improvement plan accordingly.

  • View
NITI Dashboard

A digital platform to showcase different indicators, as decided by NITI Aayog, to track and monitor the performance, which will help in identifying key focus areas, thus improving the district hospitals, and also help in developing policies and framework accordingly.

  • View
CM Dashboard

A platform to track and monitor key indicators, by the Chief Minister's office, to plan the overall health requirement in the State or focussed districts.

  • View
Comicgen

A platform to track and monitor key indicators with comics.

  • View
Program Area Dashboard

A platform to measure and track the performance of indicators within a program (e.g. Maternal Health, Finance Controller, Community Process (Asha Incentives), among others), across districts, when compared to the UP average.

It also helps District/Divisional Managers identify the reasons for low performance and help take appropriate actions, on a monthly basis.

  • View
Amethi Dashboard

Comprehensive health and nutrition block rankings for the district of Amethi. Generated based on monthly performance of all blocks across key domains such as RMNCH, NCD, CD and Nutrition.
